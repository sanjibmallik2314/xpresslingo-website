Four Blocks. One Conversation.

XpressLingo breaks every exchange into four clear blocks — so you always know what was said, what to say back, and how to say it.

Block 1

🌍 They Said

The original foreign language text appears on top — exactly as spoken — so you can verify it was heard correctly. Below it, a natural translation in your language so you understand what was asked before responding.

Block 2

💬 Suggested Response + 🎤 Your Own Words

A natural, context-appropriate response in your language — not a robotic translation, but something you'd actually say. Don't like the suggestion? Tap the 🎤 mic button to record your own words instead. XpressLingo will translate those and coach you on the phonetics too.

Block 3

🗣️ Say This in [Local Language]

The suggested response translated into the local language, written phonetically using sounds you can actually read and say — no foreign script required. Tap 🔊 to hear it spoken aloud before you attempt it yourself. Works for Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Thai, and more.

Block 4

🎤 You Said

Appears after you tap the mic in Block 2. Shows what you actually said, plus the phonetic coaching for YOUR words in the local language — so you can say it directly to the other person in their own language. Complete two-way conversation support.

Every Feature, in Depth

Everything you need to know to get the most out of XpressLingo.

🌍

Instant Language Detection

Tap once and XpressLingo listens. It uses OpenAI Whisper to identify and transcribe whatever language is being spoken — no need to specify it in advance. It handles accents, background noise, and natural speech patterns across dozens of languages.

Set Country I'm In to pre-load the expected language before anyone speaks — so XpressLingo is ready the moment a conversation starts, with zero delay.

Auto language detection 35 countries supported Works in noisy environments
💬

Two-Way Conversation Support

XpressLingo is the only travel translation app built for two-way real conversations — not just listening. After hearing what someone said, you can either use the suggested reply or tap the 🎤 mic to record your own words.

Either way, XpressLingo translates your response into the local language and writes it out phonetically — so you can actually say it yourself, not just show a screen.

Suggested OR custom reply Two-way conversation Phonetic for both directions
🗣️

Phonetic Pronunciation Coaching

Every response — whether suggested or your own words — is written out phonetically using sounds from your own language. So even in Japanese, Korean, Arabic, or Thai, you can attempt to say it yourself rather than just showing a screen.

Tap 🔊 to hear any phrase spoken aloud before you say it. This builds real confidence over repeated use — not just one-off lookups.

Phonetic in your alphabet One-tap audio playback All scripts supported
🏠

My Country — Personalised Responses

Set your home country and XpressLingo uses it in every AI response. When someone asks where you're from, the suggested reply says "I am from Canada" — not a generic placeholder.

Combined with the Country I'm In selector, XpressLingo knows both sides of the conversation — who you are and where you are — before anyone says a word.

Personalised to your origin Context-aware responses 35 home countries
📋

Conversation History & Feedback Loop

Every exchange is saved — what they said, your suggested response, what you actually said, and the phonetic coaching for both. Review it all in the History screen after your conversation or at the end of the day.

Rate each response 👍 or 👎, add a note, and suggest a better alternative. This feedback improves XpressLingo over time — making it smarter the more you travel with it.

Full conversation log Rate & improve Gets smarter over time
⏱

Flexible Recording Duration

Choose 5, 10, or 15 seconds of listening time depending on the situation. Quick tap for a short greeting; longer for directions, menu explanations, or detailed questions. Tap again at any time to stop early.

The duration setting persists across conversations — set it once for your trip and forget about it.

5 / 10 / 15 seconds Tap to stop early Persists across sessions
🔒

Privacy First

No accounts. No names. No GPS tracking. XpressLingo uses only an anonymous device ID — your conversations are yours, not ours. The app connects to our secure backend only to process audio and generate a response.

Nothing is stored beyond what you choose to keep in your own History on your device.

No account required Anonymous device ID only Secure Railway backend
