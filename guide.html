Step 1

Set Up Once

🗣️ My Language

Sets the language you read everything in — labels, suggested responses, and menus. Choose from English, Español, Français, हिंदी, 中文, Português, 日本語, বাংলা, or தமிழ்.

🌍 My Country

Tells XpressLingo where you're from, so answers about your background (e.g. if someone asks where you're visiting from) come out accurate.

📍 Country I'm In

The country you're currently traveling in. This is what lets you speak first — pick your destination and the app already knows what language to translate into.

Step 2

Have a Conversation

🎙️ Tap to Listen

The main button. Tap once to start recording — either someone speaking to you, or you speaking first. Tap again to stop early.

🔍 Detected Badge

Confirms which language is being used for this exchange, whether it came from real speech or from your "Country I'm In" pill.

🌍 They Said

The original words someone said to you, shown in their language, with the English translation underneath.

💬 Suggested Response + 🎤

A ready-made, natural-sounding reply. Don't like it? Tap the mic and record your own words in English instead — XpressLingo will translate that.

🗣️ Say This + 🔊

Shows exactly how to pronounce the response, spelled out phonetically. Tap the speaker to hear it read aloud.

🗣️ You Said + 🔊

After using the mic, this shows what you recorded and its pronunciation — a quick way to double-check before you speak.

Step 3

Fine-Tune

⏱ Recording Length

Cycle between 5, 10, and 15 second clips at the bottom of the screen. Use 5s for quick greetings, 15s for longer explanations.

Anytime

Review Your History

📜 Past Exchanges

Every conversation is saved with a timestamp and language, so you can look back at what was said.

👍 / 👎 Rate a Response

Mark a suggested response as good or needs work — this helps XpressLingo improve over time.

📝 Notes & Better Response

Add a personal note, or write in the response you wish had been suggested. Tap Save to keep your changes.
