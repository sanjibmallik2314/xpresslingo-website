How XpressLingo Works

Four steps to a real conversation in any language.

1

Tap & Listen

Tap the big button and let someone speak in their language. XpressLingo records and auto-detects what language they're speaking.

2

Instant Translation

The original text and a clear translation appear immediately — in your language, so you know exactly what was said.

3

Suggested Response

XpressLingo suggests a natural, culturally appropriate response. Tap the 🎤 mic to say your own words instead.

4

Phonetic Coaching

See and hear exactly how to say your response in their language — written in sounds you can read, spoken so you can mimic.

Everything You Need to Connect

Built for real travel. No data plan required for translation. No typing. Just speak.

🔍

Auto Language Detection

XpressLingo automatically detects what language someone is speaking — no need to select it manually. Powered by OpenAI Whisper, one of the world's most accurate speech recognition systems.

⚡

Real-Time Translation

Translation happens in seconds. The original text and English translation appear side by side so you always know exactly what was said.

🗣️

Phonetic Pronunciation

Every suggested response comes with phonetic pronunciation in sounds you can read and say — written for your native language, not generic romanisation.

🔊

Hear It Spoken

Tap the speaker button to hear the phonetic pronunciation spoken aloud. Hear it, mimic it, say it — real confidence in any conversation.

🎤

Two-Way Conversation

Tap the mic button to record your own response in your language. XpressLingo translates it into the local language phonetically so you can say it back.

🌍

Country-Aware AI

Set your home country so XpressLingo always answers "where are you from?" correctly — and tailors suggestions to your cultural context.

🗺️

9 UI Languages

The app interface itself works in English, Spanish, French, Hindi, Chinese, Portuguese, Japanese, Bengali and Tamil — so anyone can use it.

📋

Conversation History

Every exchange is saved. Review past conversations, rate responses, and help improve the AI's suggestions for your specific travel style.

🔒

Privacy First

No account required. No personal data collected. Anonymous exchanges saved to improve responses — never linked to your identity.
